Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

1. Introduction

EscoConcepts Travels ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. We comply with the Kenya Data Protection Act (DPA), 2019, which gives effect to the constitutional right to privacy under Article 31(c) and (d) of the Constitution of Kenya.

2. Data Controller

For the purposes of the DPA, EscoConcepts Travels is the Data Controller. This means we determine the purposes and means of processing your personal data. Contact details: hello@escoconceptstravels.com.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity Data: First name, last name.
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number.
  • Booking Data: Travel dates, number of guests, special requests, payment information.
  • Transaction Data: Details about your safari bookings.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage data when you interact with our website.

All personal data is collected directly from you at the point of booking or contact, in line with the DPA’s directive that data be collected from the data subject where practicable.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under Section 30 of the DPA, we process your personal data only on specific lawful grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfil your safari booking (e.g., sharing guest names, travel dates, and special requests with our safari camps, drivers, and guides to deliver the services you have booked).
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with legal requirements, such as tax or accounting laws.
  • Consent: For sending you marketing communications about new packages or special offers. You have the right to withdraw this consent at any time.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our website, prevent fraud, and ensure network security.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and confirm your safari booking.
  • Communicate with you about your itinerary and travel plans.
  • Send you a booking receipt or invoice upon request.
  • Respond to your inquiries via the contact form.
  • Send you administrative information, such as changes to our terms or policies.
  • Improve our website and customer service.

6. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the DPA, you have the following rights regarding your personal data, as outlined in Part IV of the Act:

  • Right to be Informed: You have the right to be provided with clear and transparent information about how we use your data (as provided in this policy).
  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions (e.g., where we are required to retain data for tax or legal compliance purposes). We will respond to such requests within 14 days as per the Data Protection (General) Regulations, 2021.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we stop processing your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data for direct marketing purposes.
  • Rights in relation to Automated Decision-Making and Profiling: You have the right not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing, including profiling, which produces legal effects concerning you.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@escoconceptstravels.com. We will respond to your request within the timelines prescribed by law.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your data. We may share your personal data with:

  • Service Providers: Safari camps, lodges, transport companies, and guides who are necessary to deliver your booking. These parties are data processors acting on our behalf and are contractually obligated to keep your data secure.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

8. Cross-Border Data Transfers

The DPA restricts the transfer of personal data outside Kenya unless there is proof of adequate data protection safeguards or consent from the data subject. As we work with local partners within Kenya, we do not typically transfer your data internationally. If any future international transfer is necessary, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. All data is stored on secure servers. In the event of a personal data breach that poses a real risk of harm to you, we are required by the DPA to notify the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, and will also communicate the breach to you within a reasonably practical period.

10. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this policy. For booking data, this means retaining your information for a period required by tax and accounting laws. After this period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

11. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to provide core functionality (e.g., keeping you logged in). We do not use marketing or tracking cookies at this time.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data subject rights, please contact us at:
Email: hello@escoconceptstravels.com
You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC).
