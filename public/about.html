Our Story

Born in Nairobi, told around campfires.

EscoConcepts Travels was founded over a decade ago by a small team of Kenyans who believed travel should feel personal. We design safaris like we'd design a trip for a close friend — careful, thoughtful, and full of the moments most travellers miss.

From the migration plains of the Maasai Mara to the white sands of Diani, every itinerary we craft is built on relationships: with the camps, the guides, the rangers, and the communities that call these places home.

We're proud to be locally owned, locally guided, and deeply invested in keeping the wild parts of Kenya wild.
