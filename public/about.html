Our Story

Who We Are

Welcome to EscoConcepts Travels. Founded with a deep love for the incredible landscapes and diverse cultures of Kenya, we are a premier travel agency dedicated to curating deeply personal and flawlessly executed journeys.

Whether you are seeking the thrill of the Great Migration in the Maasai Mara, a tranquil escape to the white sands of Diani, or a rugged adventure across the Great Rift Valley, our local expertise ensures your experience goes beyond the ordinary.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to connect our clients with the raw beauty of nature while providing unparalleled comfort, safety, and customer service. We believe travel should be enriching, responsible, and entirely stress-free from the moment you book until you return home.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert Local Knowledge: Our guides know the terrain, the wildlife, and the hidden gems.
  • Tailored Experiences: We recognize that no two travelers are the same, offering flexible and personalized itineraries.
  • Commitment to Sustainability: We partner with eco-friendly lodges and support local community initiatives.
